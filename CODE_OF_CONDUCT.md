# Code of Conduct

Be excellent to each other.

Disagree without being disagreeable. Engage with ideas, not personalities. Assume good faith. Apologize when wrong.

No discrimination, harassment, or hostility on the basis of any protected characteristic. Not in commits, not in issues, not in PRs, not in any project space.

Enforcement: at the maintainer's discretion. Severe or repeated violations result in a permanent ban from the project.
